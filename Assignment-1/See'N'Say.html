See'N'Say Story Teller

The See'N'Say Story Teller is a fun and interactive web application that allows users to create and listen to their own stories. By clicking on the buttons, users can select different story elements such as SOMETHNG, SOMETHING, and SILLY OUTCOMES. Once you've have made your selections, you can play back your unique story or surprise yourself with a randomly generated one. The application also includes a reset button to start fresh and create new stories whenever you like. It's a digital recreation of the classic storytelling toy by Mattel.
